Research

My work focuses on semiconductor device simulation and characterization workflows using TCAD Sentaurus (SDE/SDEVICE/S-Visual) and supporting scripts for analysis and visualization. I’m especially interested in how materials, interfaces, and quantum effects shape measurable device behavior.

Multi-Quantum Well (MQW) TCAD Device Setup
Sentaurus SDE + SDEVICE · QuantumWell physics

Built a multi-layer MQW device structure in Sentaurus SDE and configured Sentaurus Device physics with quantum well activation and hetero-interfaces. Created a repeatable simulation workflow for analyzing band behavior and charge distributions across barriers/wells.

  • Structure: scripted layered barrier/well regions (multi-well stack).
  • Physics: enabled HeteroInterface and QuantumWell models; configured plots for band edges and carrier densities.
  • Outputs: extracted band diagrams and key variables (conduction/valence band energies, charge, field, densities).

Photodetector Simulation: Optics + EQE Workflow
Sentaurus Device Optics · RayTraceBC · S-Visual analysis

Implemented an optical/electrical simulation workflow for a photodetector, including optical generation and post-processing to compute reflectance spectra, particle flux, and QE/SR.

  • Optics setup: configured OpticalGeneration with monochromatic excitation and wavelength sweeps.
  • Boundary conditions: used RayTraceBC reflectivity conditions to model optical interactions.
  • Visualization: S-Visual scripts for reflectance, flux, zero-bias QE, and responsivity.

Bandgap / PMI Parameter Study + I-V Plotting
Sentaurus Device · Bandgap models · S-Visual plotting

Created a parameterized bandgap model configuration and simulated a diode structure to study how bandgap settings influence intrinsic density, recombination behavior, and I-V response.

  • Modeling: parameterized bandgap terms and configured device physics (recombination, mobility, intrinsic density).
  • Solves: Poisson + coupled carrier solves for steady-state electrical behavior.
  • Post-processing: generated I-V curves using S-Visual scripts.

Visualization & Analysis Automation
S-Visual + Python/Pandas

Built scripts to automate plotting of device variables and band diagrams from simulation outputs (TDR/CSV), including cutline extraction and clean publication-style plots.

  • Generated energy band diagrams by extracting 1D cutlines from TDR datasets.
  • Loaded CSV exports with Pandas, computed derived quantities (e.g., bandgap), and created S-Visual variables for plotting.
